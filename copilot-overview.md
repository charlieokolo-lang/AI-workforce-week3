Microsoft Copilot is an AI-powered assistant developed by Microsoft that is embedded across its ecosystem of products and services to enhance productivity, automate tasks, and support decision-making. It uses large language models (LLMs) combined with your organisational data to generate contextually relevant responses, drafts, summaries, and insights.

Copilot depends on identity - it operates within the context of a signed-in user, meaning it only acts on behalf of that authenticated individual and respects their access boundaries. 

It depends on data - Copilot draws from the content and information the user has access to, such as emails, documents, meetings, and chats stored within Microsoft 365.

It depends on permissions — Copilot strictly adheres to existing role-based access controls, meaning it will never surface data that the user does not already have permission to view.
